3. Some export/report labels remain AED-specific by design

I still see many Total (AED), VAT (AED), Subtotal (AED) labels across reports and exports.

Examples include:

server/routes/system.ts
client/src/pages/Invoices.tsx
client/src/pages/Quotations.tsx
client/src/components/reports/StatementsTab.tsx
client/src/components/reports/PaymentsLedger.tsx
client/src/components/reports/PoGrnReport.tsx
client/src/pages/PurchaseOrders.tsx
My judgment

Most of these appear intentional because those screens are using AED-normalized values, not original-currency values.

So I am not flagging these as bugs by default.

The important distinction is:

where the app is showing a true AED-converted value → label is fine
where the app is showing document-native currency → label must not say AED

That is why the Delivery Order print/export inconsistency stands out more than the reports.
